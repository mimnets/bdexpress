# 01 — Business Model Overview

## What We're Building

A **cross-border e-commerce & sourcing platform** that enables Bangladeshi consumers, SMEs, and F-commerce (Facebook commerce) sellers to:

1. **Browse & discover products** from Chinese wholesale platforms (Taobao, 1688, Alibaba, Tmall)
2. **Place orders** through our unified platform — either from our curated catalog or by pasting Chinese product links
3. **Pay in BDT** (bKash, Nagad, Rocket, cards, bank transfer)
4. **Get products delivered door-to-door** — our China team handles: purchase, quality check, warehousing, consolidation, international shipping, customs clearance, and last-mile delivery in Bangladesh

## Core Value Proposition

| Pain Point | Our Solution |
|------------|-------------|
| Chinese platforms are in Mandarin — can't read listings | We translate and verify product details |
| Need Chinese bank card / Alipay to pay | We pay in RMB; customers pay in BDT |
| Chinese suppliers don't ship overseas | We consolidate at our China warehouse |
| Customs clearance is complex & risky | We handle all documentation & clearance |
| No quality check before shipping | QC inspection & photos at China warehouse |
| Fragmented shipping — high costs | Consolidation = lower per-unit shipping |
| No trust in unknown suppliers | We vet and verify suppliers |

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Service Fee (Commission)
- **For order-by-order (individual buyers):** 5–10% commission on product cost
- **For bulk/B2B clients:** 3–5% commission (volume-based tiered pricing)
- **For F-commerce/dropshipping:** 8–12% (includes product sourcing + image support)

### 2. Shipping & Logistics Margin
- Markup on international shipping (air/sea/express)
- Domestic last-mile delivery fee
- Warehousing & consolidation fee

### 3. Currency Exchange Spread
- Margin on RMB-to-BDT conversion (small, competitive spread)

### 4. Premium Services
- **QC Video Inspection:** Extra fee for detailed quality inspection video
- **Custom Branding/Packaging:** For bulk B2B clients
- **Sourcing Consultation:** Paid consultation for large import orders
- **Express Handling:** Priority processing fee

### 5. Platform Monetization ( Later Phase)
- Featured product listings for suppliers
- Advertising/sponsored products on platform
- Subscription plans for frequent B2B buyers

## Business Model Flowchart

```
User browses platform
       │
       ├──> Links from Taobao/1688/Alibaba
       │         │
       │         └──> Our system parses & fetches product details via API
       │
       ├──> Our curated catalog
       │
       └──> Manual request (screenshot/description)
                │
                ▼
        User adds to cart & checks out
                │
                ▼
        Payment in BDT (bKash/Nagad/Rocket/Cards)
                │
                ▼
        Notification to China team (Touab)
                │
                ▼
        Purchase from Chinese supplier
                │
                ▼
        Supplier delivers to China warehouse
                │
                ▼
        Quality inspection & photos
                │
                ▼
        Consolidation (multi-order) + Packing
                │
                ▼
        International Shipping: Air (7-12 days) / Sea (20-40 days)
                │
                ▼
        Bangladesh customs clearance
                │
                ▼
        Last-mile delivery to customer
                │
                ▼
        Customer receives order + tracking updates throughout
```
