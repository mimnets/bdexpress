## Marketing & Growth Strategy

### Pre-Launch (Weeks 1–6)
- Facebook group for "China import from Bangladesh" — build community
- YouTube educational content (how to import, product reviews)
- Touab's existing client network — warm introductions
- SEO content (bangla articles about China importing)

### Launch (Weeks 7–10)
- Facebook & Instagram ads (targeting: small business owners, F-commerce sellers)
- Launch discount: first order free shipping
- Referral program: refer a friend, both get BDT 200 off
- Facebook Live event: Q&A about the platform

### Growth (Month 3–6)
- Partner with BD YouTubers/influencers in e-commerce niche
- F-commerce seller partnerships
- College/university ambassador program
- WhatsApp broadcast marketing

### Scale (Month 6+)
- Google Ads for high-intent keywords
- B2B trade show participation
- Strategic partnerships with Daraz sellers
- Press releases in BD business media
