## Competitor 1: MoveOn Global

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Website** | <https://moveon.global/BD_en> |
| **Headquarters** | Global (multiple countries) |
| **Markets** | Bangladesh, Pakistan, Global |
| **App** | Yes (iOS + Android) |
| **Founded** | ~2023 (est.) |

### Services Offered
- **Ship for Me Request** — Buy Taobao/1688 links
- **RFQ (Request for Quotation)** — Bulk sourcing
- **Talk to Expert** — Personalized sourcing consultation
- **Cost Calculator** — Estimate shipping costs
- **Buy Order** — Direct purchase from their catalog
- **Multi-country sourcing** — China, USA, UAE, India, Pakistan, Bangladesh

### Key Strengths
- Sophisticated platform with CMS backend (Strapi-based)
- Multi-country operations (not just China → BD)
- Professional UI/UX
- Has product inventory from multiple countries
- Wholesale & retail pricing tiers
- Campaign/slider marketing features

### Weaknesses
- **No local payment support** — doesn't accept bKash/Nagad/Rocket
- Less focused on Bangladesh-specific user needs
- No F-commerce/dropshipping specific tools
- No Bengali language interface (English only)
- No AI/automation features visible
- No Telegram/Facebook bot ordering

### Estimated Traffic & Scale
- Multiple region-specific subdomains
- Active social media presence
- YouTube channel with shipping tutorials

| **Legal Entity** | Laobaan Bangladesh Ltd. |
| **Phone** | +8801811509999 |

| **Social** | Facebook, YouTube |

### Services Offered
- China product sourcing & shipping solutions
- Curated product catalog (shoes, bags, jewelry, watches, apparel)
- Video gallery with educational content (Bangla)
- Bengali-language content throughout

### Key Strengths
- **Strong local presence** — Physical office in Uttara, Dhaka
- **Localized content** — Bengali language, culturally relevant
- **Local payment** — bKash/Nagad/Rocket integration likely
- **Educational content** — YouTube videos teaching customers how to import
- **Android app** in Play Store
- **Registered BD company** — builds trust

### Weaknesses
- **Website quality is basic** — looks like a template/custom setup
- **No iOS app** (only Android)
- **Limited product catalog** compared to MoveOn
- **No real-time search** across Chinese platforms
- **No API-based sourcing** — likely manual processing
- **No automation** for customer support
- **No B2B wholesale portal** visible
- **No F-commerce integration**

### Estimated Scale
- Small-medium operation
- Local team handling orders manually
- Focused on individual consumer orders
- Educational YouTube content driving traffic

| **Alibaba.com Direct** | B2B direct sourcing | Low — requires English, card, customs knowledge |
| **Superbuy/CSSBuy** | Global China agents | Low — not BD focused, no local payment |

## Key Market Insights

1. **F-commerce is massive in Bangladesh** — thousands of Facebook-based sellers who import from China but have no streamlined platform. Our biggest opportunity.
2. **Trust is the #1 barrier** — Bangladeshis are cautious about paying upfront for China imports. A physical office + BD company registration + references are essential.
3. **Education sells** — Both competitors use educational content (YouTube videos, blog posts). Customers need to understand "how it works."
4. **Price sensitivity is high** — Shipping cost is the deciding factor. A transparent cost calculator is critical.
5. **Mobile-first market** — Over 90% of BD users access the internet via mobile. Mobile app + mobile web is non-negotiable.
6. **Seasonal spikes** — Eid-ul-Fitr, Eid-ul-Adha, Pohela Boishakh drive massive import demand.
