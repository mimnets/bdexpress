## End-to-End Order Fulfillment Process

```
STEP 1: CUSTOMER PLACES ORDER
├── Via Website / App / Telegram Bot / Facebook
├── Type: Link from Taobao/1688 OR Curated product OR Manual request
└── System generates quote & sends to customer

STEP 2: PAYMENT RECEIVED
├── Customer pays via bKash / Nagad / Rocket / Card / Bank
├── Admin verifies payment (automated in Phase 2)
└── Order status → "Paid — Processing"

STEP 3: CHINA PROCUREMENT (Touab's Team)
├── Purchase product from Taobao/1688/Alibaba supplier
├── Supplier delivers to China warehouse
├── Quality check & photos taken
├── If issues → Contact customer for resolution
└── Order status → "In Warehouse — QC Done"

STEP 4: CONSOLIDATION & PACKING
├── Combine multiple customer items into one parcel
├── Professional packing for international shipping
├── Label with tracking number
└── Order status → "Ready for Shipping"

STEP 5: INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING
├── Air Freight: 7–12 days (under 20kg, urgent)
├── Sea Freight LCL: 20–40 days (large orders, cost-effective)
├── Express Courier: 5–8 days (DHL/FedEx — premium)
└── Order status → "In Transit"

STEP 6: BANGLADESH CUSTOMS CLEARANCE
├── Documentation prepared (commercial invoice, packing list, HS code)
├── Duties & taxes paid on behalf of customer
├── Customs broker handles clearance
└── Order status → "Customs Clearance"

STEP 7: LAST-MILE DELIVERY
├── Hand over to BD courier partner
├── Customer notified with local tracking
├── Delivery to doorstep
└── Order status → "Delivered"

STEP 8: POST-DELIVERY
├── Customer satisfaction call/message
├── Request review/testimonial
└── Upsell for next order
```

## Touab's China Warehouse Operations

### Warehouse Requirements
- **Location:** Near major supplier hubs (Guangzhou/Shenzhen/Yiwu preferred)
- **Space:** Minimum 200–500 sq ft to start
- **Staff:** 1–2 people for receiving, inspection, packing, shipping
- **Equipment:** Shelving, packing materials, scale, camera for QC photos

### Warehouse Workflow
```
Goods Arrive → Check against order → QC Inspection →
  → Photos taken → Update system → Consolidation →
    → Packing → Labeling → Dispatch to carrier
```

### Quality Control Checklist
- [ ] Product matches listing (model, color, size, quantity)
- [ ] No visible damage
- [ ] Functional test (for electronics)
- [ ] Photos taken (3+ angles)
- [ ] Weight verified
- [ ] Packaging suitable for international shipping

## Shipping Methods & Pricing Strategy

| Method | Weight | Delivery Time | Cost (per kg to BD) | Best For |
|--------|--------|---------------|---------------------|----------|
| **Air Freight** | 0.5–20 kg | 7–12 days | $5–12/kg | Small orders, electronics, fashion |
| **Sea Freight (LCL)** | 20–500 kg | 25–40 days | $2–5/kg | Bulk orders, furniture, heavy items |
| **Express (DHL/FedEx)** | 0.5–50 kg | 5–8 days | $8–18/kg | Urgent, high-value items |
| **Sea Freight (FCL)** | Full container | 30–45 days | $800–2000/container | Large businesses only |

### Pricing Model for Customers
```
Total Customer Cost = 
  Product Price (CNY → BDT) 
  + Service Fee (5–12% of product price)
  + International Shipping (per kg, method-based)
  + Customs Duties & Taxes (actual, passed through)
  + Last-mile Delivery (fixed BDT fee)
```

## Bangladesh Customs & Compliance

### Required Documentation
1. Commercial Invoice
2. Packing List
3. Bill of Lading / Airway Bill
4. Import Registration Certificate (IRC) — for commercial importers
5. Customs Bond / Bank Guarantee — for high-value items

### Duty & Tax Structure (BD)
- **Import Duty:** 5–25% depending on HS code
- **VAT:** 15% on (CIF + Duty)
- **Advance Income Tax (AIT):** 4–5%
- **Regulatory Duty (RD):** 3–5% on certain items
- **Total Effective Rate:** Typically 25–60% of CIF value

### Prohibited / Restricted Items
- Used clothing (unless specific license)
- Certain electronics without BTRC approval
- Food products without BSTI clearance
- Counterfeit / trademark infringing goods
- Lithium batteries (restricted shipping)
